LOCATION

Round Barn Farm is a historic destination in beautiful Waitsfield, Vermont. The Joslyn Round Barn was built in 1910 and served as a working dairy barn for nearly 60 years. The concept of a “round barn” originated with the Shakers, who built 12-sided barns because they believed the "devil couldn’t catch you in the corners.” The Joslyn Round Barn is one of only five remaining round barns in Vermont.

DIRECTIONS

Shuttles to and from select inns, B&Bs, and hotels will be provided. Details can be found on the accommodations page.
Free parking will be available across the street from the Inn. Handicap parking is available if needed.

From the Burlington International Airport

30 miles, 40 minutes

Exit the airport Left on Rt. 2 South. Proceed approximately 2.5 miles to Rt. 2A. Turn Right, and drive less than 1 mile to the highway. Turn Left at the second entrance, heading South on I-89 to Exit 10 (Waterbury). Take Rt. 100 South. Travel 12 miles to Historic Waitsfield Village. At the old village center, take a Left onto Bridge Street and travel through the covered bridge. Bridge street then becomes East Warren Road. The Inn will be 2 miles up the road on the Left.

From Boston

190 miles, 3.5 hours

Leave the Boston area on I-93 North. In Concord, NH connect with I-89 North. Travel 120 miles through NH and Vermont. After passing Montpelier, take Exit 9 (Middlesex) off I-89. The Inn at the Round Barn is a total of 15 miles from Exit 9. Travel Rt. 100B South, continuing on 100 South. Travel 12 miles to Historic Waitsfield Village. At the old village center, take a Left onto Bridge Street and travel through the covered bridge. Bridge street then becomes East Warren Road. The Inn will be 2 miles up the road on the Left.

From New York City

300 miles, 6 hours

Leave the NY area on the NY State Thruway 87 North to Albany, NY. The NY State Thruway, I-87, connects to the Northway near Albany. Take the Northway to Exit 20. The sign indicates Rt. 149 towards Fort Ann. Turn Left off the ramp and onto Rt. 149 towards Fort Ann. Stay on 149 through Fort Ann to Whitehall.

In Whitehall, NY turn right onto Rt. 4 at the traffic light. Take Rt. 4 North through to Vermont. Drive 1.5 miles and then take Rt. 22A North. Follow Rt. 22A for about 38 miles (very pretty route) and turn Right onto Rt. 17 East. Follow Rt. 17 East until it ends in Waitsfield. Turn Left onto Rt. 100 and travel North approximately 2 miles. At the old village center, turn Right onto Bridge Street and travel through the covered bridge. Bridge Street then becomes East Warren Road. The Inn will be 2 miles up the road on the Left.
