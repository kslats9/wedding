Our Story

THE TIMELINE

Kevin and Katie have shared five years of love, joy, and adventure. Here's a quick walk down memory lane.
FEB
2013
THE MEET CUTE

It was a warm, sunny Super Bowl Sunday in San Francisco. The year was 2013. The Niners were eager to take on the Ravens, Beyonce was ready to slay the halftime show, and Oreo was poised to make social media history during the famous blackout.

Katie was watching the game with her friend Megan at Megan's boyfriend Mike's apartment. Katie, per usual, was scoping out the food situation. Just as she was trying to decide which of the store bought chips and dips to eat, a cute guy wearing an Illinois t-shirt walked through the door carrying a tray of fresh cherry tomatoes he had stuffed with cheese and herbs. She instinctively threw out her hand to introduce herself (and grab a few tomatoes), and that was the first time Kevin and Katie met.

They spent the game talking about food, traveling (Kevin had just gotten back from a trip to Europe with his friends), San Francisco, and Beyonce. They exchanged numbers at the end of the night, and the rest was history.
SEP
2014
THE JOINING OF MUG COLLECTIONS

In the fall of 2014, they moved into their first apartment together in the NOPA neighborhood of San Francisco.

Since then, they've been growing their collections of kitchen tools, big mugs, and vinyl for their record player, and they spend a lot of time fantastizing about more counter space and bigger closets. They've also been eating and drinking their way through San Francisco, the great state of California, and beyond.
AUG
2017
THE PROPOSAL

On August 5, 2017, Kevin and Katie decided to grab dinner at their favorite San Francisco seafood joint, Woodhouse Fish Company. Afterwards, they washed their buttery crab rolls down with brews from one of their favorite beer bars, Monk's Kettle. Katie was ready to grab a Lyft home, but much to her chagrin, Kevin insisted they walk. Their stroll took them through magical Duboce Park, where they used to hang out every weekend when they first started dating; the park was in between their old apartments and filled to the brim with the cutest pups you could ever imagine.

They decided to sit on a bench and enjoy the unseasonably warm evening. As they were sitting there, Kevin told Katie how much he loved her, and how he was excited to spend the rest of his life with her. Clueless, Katie started babbling on about how she loved him too, how she was excited about their future Bernedoodle, that she couldn't wait to have a house with a double vanity in the bathroom, and so on... when Kevin suddenly got down on not one, but two knees (he maintains it was a positioning issue), pulled a beautiful ring box out of his pocket, and asked her to marry him. It was at this point that Katie FINALLY caught on, squealed a bit, and said yes!

SEP
2018

THE WEDDING

Kevin and Katie are incredibly excited to tie the knot and kick off the next chapter in their lives in September by celebrating with their amazing families and friends in Waitsfield, VT!
