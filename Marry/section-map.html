  • Check out the Cold Hollow Cider Mill for amazing apple cider and apple cider donuts.
  • Plan a roadtrip through New England! Peak fall foliage typically begins the last week in September and lasts for the first couple of weeks in October, so hopefully you'll catch the start of the leaves changing!
