ACCOMODATIONS

One of our favorite things about Vermont is the abundance of warm, welcoming B&Bs serving up hot, delicious breakfast! We recommend any of the below options, all of which are within a 10-15 minute drive of Round Barn Farm. We've also included a few large Airbnb's for those of you looking to stay in a house with a group of family or friends. We will be working with a local shuttle company to coordinate a route to pick guests up before the wedding and drop them off afterwards. Please select lodging within the 10-15 minute radius of Round Barn Farm if you're interested in partaking in the shuttle service.

Fall is a popular time in Vermont, so we recommend booking your accommodations sooner rather than later!

Hotels

Pros: Closer to Burlington, the Ben & Jerry’s Factory, and Cold Hollow Cider Mill, plus we’ll be organizing shuttles to/from both hotels
Cons: Farther from Waitsfield, a bit more expensive

Inns and B&Bs

Pros: Closer to venue, quintessential Vermont B&B experience, more affordable options, home-cooked breakfasts
Cons: Most have two-night minimums; we’ll only be organizing shuttles to/from select locations

Airbnb and VRBO

Pros: Large groups can stay together, significantly more affordable, ideal for longer stays
Cons: Neither breakfast nor transportation to/from wedding are provided
