WHERE TO STAY

One of our favorite things about Vermont is the abundance of warm, welcoming B&Bs serving up hot, delicious breakfast! We recommend any of the below options, most of which are within a 10-15 minute drive of Round Barn Farm. We've also included a few VRBOs for those of you looking to stay in a house with a group of family or friends. We'll be working with a local shuttle company to coordinate pick-ups and drop-offs at select locations before and after the wedding.

Our wedding weekend is an extremely popular time in Vermont. We strongly recommend booking your accommodations sooner rather than later!

Inns and B&Bs

Hyde Away Inn (afterparty location + we’ll provide shuttles)
1428 Mill Brook Road, Waitsfield, VT 05673 (4.4 miles, or a 9 minute drive)
802-496-2322
Rooms start at $139/night.
We’ve reserved the entire inn for our wedding weekend, and this is where the afterparty will be on Saturday night! The Hyde Away offers casual, comfy lodging with a great tavern and restaurant. There are 10 lodging options ranging from large rooms to retro ski dorm rooms over the Tavern to the Hyde Away House. All rooms have full- to queen-sized beds, include a full country breakfast, and require a two-night minimum stay.

The Waitsfield Inn (we’ll provide shuttles)
5267 Main St, Waitsfield, VT 05673 (2.6 miles, or a 5 minute drive)
802-496-3979
Rooms start at $125/night.
The Waitsfield Inn has 12 guest rooms offering the comfort, tradition, and warm hospitality of a classic Vermont country inn. Each room has a private bath, and there's a crackling fire in the Great Room and a library filled with books to peruse. There's also a full service bar, and complimentary WiFi throughout the inn. You'll receive a breakfast with your stay.

Yellow Farmhouse Inn (we’ll provide shuttles)
550 Old County Rd, Waitsfield, VT 05673 (2.4 miles, or a 5 minute drive)
877-257-5767
Rooms start at $149/night.
Enjoy the feeling of a traditional New England Bed & Breakfast at the Yellow Farmhouse Inn. There are 8 spacious rooms to choose from, all of which have a maximum occupancy of 2 people. Morning breakfast is included!

The Warren Lodge
731 VT Rt. 100, Warren, VT 05674 (6.1 miles, or a 10 minute drive)
802-496-3084
Rooms start at $139/night.
The Warren Lodge is a gorgeous property with newly remodeled guest rooms. Room options range from standard to "efficiency units" with kitchenettes, and there's also a guest house. Breakfast is NOT included in your stay here, and we won’t be organizing shuttles from this location.

Featherbed Inn (we’ll provide shuttles)
5864 Main Street, Rte. 100 Waitsfield, VT 05673 (3.2 miles, or a 7 minute drive)
802-496-7151
Rooms start at $130/night.
The Featherbed Inn has nine spacious bedrooms that can accommodate 2-4 people each. All but one room has an in-room bathroom; the bathroom for the final room is across the hall from the room. All rates include a full breakfast and afternoon refreshments.

Wilder Farm Inn (we’ll provide shuttles)
1460 Main St, Waitsfield, VT 05673 (4.6 miles, or a 7 minute drive)
800-496-8878
Rooms start at $142/night.
At the Wilder Farm Inn, every square foot is ever changing with decoration. This New England Bed & Breakfast offers 8 guest rooms with different accommodation styles, ranging from 2-4 occupancies. Breakfast is included in your room rate.

West Hill House B&B
1496 W Hill Rd, Warren, VT 05674 (9.1 miles, or a 15 minute drive)
802-496-7162
Rooms start at $160/night.
West Hill House B&B has nine guest rooms with private baths, and they offer piping hot coffee and a three course breakfast each morning during your stay. Relax in the common areas or explore the grounds in your down time, and don't forget to enjoy the home baked goods and hot beverages that are available at all times. We won’t be organizing shuttles from this location.

Weathertop Mountain Inn
755 Mill Brook Rd, Waitsfield, VT 05673 (3.7 miles, or an 8 minute drive)
800-800-3625
Rooms start at $139/night.
Behind the deceptively simple, cedar-planked facade of this European-style mountain chalet, visitors to the Weathertop Mountain Inn will find 8 tastefully decorated guest rooms, each with a private bath. The inn has a large common area and game room in addition to an indoor jacuzzi and Finnish sauna. We won’t be organizing shuttles from this location.

Buffalo Bill's Retreat
64 Wildfire Dr, Warren, VT 05674 (8.6 miles, or a 16 minute drive)
802-496-6942
The 5-room chalet is $648/night, the 4-room chalet is $498/night, and the studio is $178/night.
Buffalo Bill's Retreat is a great option for groups of two to twelve looking for their own property. There are 5- and 4-room chalets, and a studio. These properties are adjacent to Sugarbush, meaning they offer beautiful mountain views and wooded surroundings. Each unit is fully furnished and equipped with woodstoves, spatial decks, and outdoor hot tubs, and all of them come with linens, towels, and all of the amenities of home. Breakfast is NOT provided, and we won’t be organizing shuttles from this location.

Pitcher Inn
275 Main St, Warren, VT 05674 (7.8 miles, or a 14 minute drive)
802-496-6350
Rooms start at $375/night.
Looking for a serious splurge? The Pitcher Inn is a luxury B&B in Warren, VT. Each of the two suites and nine rooms is designed by a different artist, and daily breakfast and afternoon tea is included in your room rate. We won’t be organizing shuttles from this location.

Transportation

Burlington Limo and Car Service
802-288-9810, limo@sover.net
Large fleet selection to meet any need. Price and time vary based on event, numbers, time, and distance. They’re part of the Middlebury Transportation Group.

Alpine Limo
802-793-3543
They have one 14-passenger bus. Rates are flexible and based upon variables such as the nature of your trip, number of passengers, wait time, and mileage driven. Consumption of alcohol is allowed on the bus by persons of legal age; they won’t purchase or provide alcohol, but can provide coolers, ice, snacks etc. upon request.

Ethan Allen Coachworks
802-862-1924
Private town car service, group passenger van service, luxury SUV limousines seating 10-14, full stretch limousines seating up to 10 (restrictions apply), a Cadillac Escalade, and a full-size Suburban available. Price and time vary based on event, numbers, time, and distance.

Valet Park America

Provides transportation services around Mad River Valley.
